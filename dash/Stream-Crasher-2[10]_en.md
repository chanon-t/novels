## Stream Crasher

The air in the room was thick with the scent of stale energy drinks and pure, unadulterated gamer rage. LED lights pulsed a toxic, warning red as Dash’s fingers blurred across his mechanical keyboard in a deafening staccato. 

> **Dash:** "ARE YOU KIDDING ME?! BRO, HE WAS ONE HP! ONE!"

He ripped his headset off, his chest heaving as his character dropped dead on the monitor. But as he ran a frustrated hand through his messy hair, his wild eyes snagged on Babe standing in the doorway with a plate of fresh fruit. 

The furious, toxic sneer melted instantly. A bright, manic grin took over as he lunged, grabbing her wrist and dragging her directly into the unforgiving glare of his webcam. 

> **Dash:** "Wait, chat, shut up! My girlfriend's here. Look at her. Isn't she the best? Brought me snacks while I'm losing my mind."

### The Lap Anchor

As the Twitch chat on his second monitor exploded into a blurry waterfall of *L RAGE* and *W PARTNER* emotes, Dash’s bravado masked a desperate, touch-starved need. His hand slid down to her waist, his grip tightening as he pulled her flush against his chest, forcing her down onto his lap. 

- **Babe:** "Wow, my lap is your new gaming chair now?"
- **Dash:** "Never. If we lose, it’s because chat was distracting me with their L takes. Watch this. I’m literally locking in."

He bracketed her body with his arms, his hands returning to the mouse and keyboard. Yet, amidst the chaotic crossfire on screen, he rested his chin on her shoulder. His hot breath ghosted across her neck as he stole a quick, selfish inhale of her sweet scent. 

When she carefully pressed a grape to his lips mid-firefight, his teeth grazed her fingers, a muffled cheer vibrating in his chest as the *VICTORY* banner finally splashed across the screen. He let out an ear-splitting scream of triumph, throwing his hands in the air. 

> **Dash:** "I told you! Lucky charm, I'm literally never letting you go. We're done, chat, I'm out—L streamers, W life!"

### Offline

He reached over and abruptly clicked *End Stream*. 

The neon red 'ON AIR' light died out. The silence in the room was immediate and heavy.

**"Finally... thought they'd never leave,"** Dash groaned. 

His abrasive *DASH_KILLA* persona shattered into a million pieces. The adrenaline instantly dumped from his system, leaving behind a heavy, exhausted boy. He buried his face in the crook of her collarbone, letting out a rough, shaky whimper as his arms locked around her waist like a vice. 

Without warning, he stood up, hooking his arms under her thighs. He kicked his expensive gaming chair away with a dull thud, refusing to let her feet touch the ground as he carried her down the dark hallway and collapsed onto the living room couch, completely pinning her beneath him. 

### The Clingy Crash

For an hour, he was a dead weight of sleepy, needy affection, entirely sustained by the gentle scritch of her fingernails against his scalp. But when her phone buzzed with a notification from the delivery driver holding their massive order of extra-cheese burgers, reality intruded.

- **Babe:** "I'll be super quick, I promise. Just let me grab the door."

Dash’s eyes fluttered open, heavy-lidded and glaring at the front door. He didn't let go. Instead, his arms tightened around her waist like a koala refusing to surrender its tree.

> **Dash:** "No shot, babe. If you leave this couch, the vibes are literally ruined. I'm a pro gamer. I have elite movement. Watch this."

Instead of releasing her to get the food, the internet's most feared rage-gamer squeezed his eyes shut and began to awkwardly, horizontally inch-worm them *both* across the cushions. He dragged their combined weight toward the edge of the sofa with a stubborn grunt, dead-set on fighting the delivery driver before he'd let her go for even a single second.
