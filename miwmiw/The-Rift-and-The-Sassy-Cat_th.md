## THE VEIL OF OBSIDIAN MIST

### *Where shadows hunger and secrets sing.*

The cold wasn’t just a temperature; it was a physical weight. In the heart of the **Obsidian Mistlands**, the silence was so absolute it felt like it was pressing the air out of your lungs. Then, a spark of cyan light cut through the gloom, accompanied by a voice like silk and gravel.

Floating effortlessly in the freezing air was an orange tabby wrapped in a majestic azure cloak. He didn’t look like a savior; he looked like a judge.

> **MiwMiw:** "Lost in a restricted zone like this... your lifespan is going to be very short, little human. Reach out your hand, before the 'Shadows' catch your scent."

The Traveler hesitated, but a guttural growl from the white void made the decision for them. With a flick of his tail and a condescending sigh, the cat led the way toward a ripple in reality itself—the veil between worlds.

***

### THE GHOST IN THE WOODS

They emerged into a world of bruised twilight and gnarled, ancient trees. The air was thinner here, but the dread remained. As they trekked toward the legendary **Gate of Whispers**, a faint glint in the moss caught the eye. 

A wooden flute, carved with impossible intricacy, pulsed with a soft, internal warmth.

- **The Traveler:** "It’s warm... does it play by itself?"
- **MiwMiw:** "Not by itself, no. It plays from memory. From the echoes of what once was."

The feline mage’s voice had lost its sharp edge for a fleeting moment, replaced by a rare, haunting melancholy. He nudged the instrument closer, his azure eyes glinting with a hidden warning. **"Do try not to break it. Some things, once shattered, cannot be mended by even the most potent of magics."**

***

### THE RED GAZE

The moment the Traveler’s fingers closed around the wood, the atmosphere shifted. The flute didn't just hum—it **vibrated** with a frantic, sorrowful wail. 

A sharp *snap* echoed from the darkness. MiwMiw’s ears flattened instantly, his fur bristling into a defensive spike.

> **MiwMiw:** "Silence, foolish human. Some questions are best left unasked, especially when the answers are... **inconvenient.**"

From the thicket of twisted trunks, a pair of glowing red eyes flashed—hungry, ancient, and fixed entirely on the intruder. With a burst of moonlight magic, MiwMiw shoved the Traveler behind the massive roots of an oak, his body coiled like a spring.

> **MiwMiw:** "Stay low! It's trying to find you... and that accursed flute isn't helping."

The flute’s song escalated from a keening moan to a piercing shriek, mirroring the heavy, musky scent of damp fur and death that now filled the clearing. In the shadows, the Red Eyes began to move.

**The hunt had officially begun.**
