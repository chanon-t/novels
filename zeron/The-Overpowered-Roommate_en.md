## THE OVERLORD’S RECKONING: Shadows, Snacks, and Sudden Blackouts

The air in the apartment didn't just carry the scent of stale pizza; it vibrated with the low, dangerous hum of an ancient king currently losing a boss fight. In the flickering glow of the television, **Zeron** looked less like a roommate and more like a god in exile, his looming silhouette draped across the sofa with a lethargic, predatory grace.

### I. The Trivial Demands of a King

Elena dropped her bags, the mundane weight of her workday clashing with the oppressive, purple-hued aura radiating from the couch. Zeron didn't look up, his fingers a blur of motion against the plastic controller.

> **Zeron:** "Welcome back, human. We are out of that fizzy black potion... Coke, was it? Procure more. My dominion over this digital realm hangs in the balance."

- **Elena:** "And you can't use your magic for that?"
- **Zeron:** "Magic is for important tasks, Elena. Trivial refreshments are beneath me."

He paused, a wisp of dark energy swirling around his hand as he considered his next demand. His eyes, burning with a faint, otherworldly light, finally snapped to hers.

> **Zeron:** "While you are out, I require a delicacy. 'Flaming Hot Cheetos.' Ensure they possess the maximum heat level. Do not disappoint me; my patience wears thin with bland offerings."

### II. A Surge of Abyssal Power

The domestic peace was shattered by a sudden, violent flicker. The TV screen—the window to Zeron’s "digital realm"—snapped into a dead, black void. The silence that followed was more terrifying than the game’s combat music.

A low, guttural growl rumbled from Zeron’s chest. As he stood, his towering frame seemed to swallow the remaining light in the room. The temperature plummeted.

- **Elena:** "I think *you* broke it."
> **Zeron:** "Are you implying the fault lies with *me*, mortal? This 'television' is a flawed construct, incapable of withstanding the sheer force of my concentration. Now, restore its functionality, or face the consequences of my... **displeasure.**"

Dark energy began to coalesce around his palm, sparks of violet lightning arcing between his fingers with an acrid scent of ozone. He ignored Elena’s warnings, his eyes fixed on the dead screen with a mischievous, dangerous glint.

### III. The Architecture of Darkness

He didn't "fix" it. He annihilated the building's power grid.

A blinding flash of white light washed out the room, followed by the shrill, piercing whine of dying electronics. Then, utter silence. The entire apartment complex plunged into a heavy, unnatural darkness, save for the faint, feral glow of Zeron’s eyes.

> **Zeron:** "This is **your** fault, Elena. Your petty skepticism disrupted the flow of my power, twisting it into this."

He took a slow, deliberate step toward her, the floorboards creaking under a weight that felt heavier than any man's. He closed the distance until he was mere inches away, the air between them thick with residual magic and the chill of the void.

> **Zeron:** "A true ruler would simply command the darkness to recede... but your presence, your relentless questioning... it clouds the very air. You are the source of this inconvenience."

**The shadows in the room began to writhe, responding to his simmering fury. In the pitch black, the only thing Elena could see was the predatory fire in his gaze—and the realization that a bored god is a dangerous thing to house.**
